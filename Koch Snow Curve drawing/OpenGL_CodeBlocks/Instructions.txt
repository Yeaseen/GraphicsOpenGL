Camera is at negative Z-axis(0,0,-100) and looking to positive Z-axis. u is Y-axis, r is X-axis.



If pressing any of these:
1 - rotate/look left
2 - rotate/look right
3 - look up
4 - look down
5 - tilt clockwise
6 - tilt counterclockwise


up arrow - move forward
down arrow - move backward
right arrow - move right
left arrow - move left
PgUp - move up
PgDn - move down
